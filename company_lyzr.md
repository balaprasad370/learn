# Lyzr AI — Company Dossier

Researched: 2026-04-23

## Snapshot

- **What they do:** Full-stack agentic AI platform — low-code framework to design, deploy, and govern AI agents with pre-built RAG pipelines. Pitch: "Deploy AI agents to production in weeks, not quarters." Enterprise focus.
- **HQ:** New Jersey, USA (registered) + Bengaluru, India (engineering base).
- **Stage:** Series A.
- **Total funding:** $37.6M across 7 rounds from 9 investors.
- **Latest round:** March 2026, led by **Accenture**. Valuation quintupled to **$250M**.
- **Headcount:** ~142 employees (Feb 2026).
- **Open positions:** 12–30 depending on source (careers.lyzr.ai shows ~20, Uplers mirrors 10 eng roles).
- **Product layer:** agent framework (OSS on GitHub: LyzrCore/lyzr-framework), enterprise platform, Lyzr Studio, safety/responsible-AI modules.

## Verdict for your target (5 YoE, ~50 LPA, AI/ML + full-stack + DevOps)

**Strong fit.** Multiple roles match your exact stack — Python + FastAPI + Next.js/React + AWS/Docker + LLM/RAG. Accenture-led round at $250M valuation means fresh cash for hiring + meaningful equity. Remote options exist for several roles.

**Caveat:** Lyzr does **not** publish comp bands anywhere public (careers page, Uplers, Wellfound, Glassdoor). You'll need to extract the range via recruiter call. For an Indian AI Series A at this valuation, a realistic senior band is **₹45–80 LPA cash + 0.1–0.5% ESOP** for a 5–8 YoE engineer — but confirm, do not assume.

## Red flags to watch

- Many of their engineering roles are **Bengaluru on-site only**, including the top match (Senior Core Platform Engineer). If fully remote is non-negotiable for you, focus on the 3 remote roles.
- Public comp silence is typical for Indian AI startups at this stage but means more negotiation work on your end.
- Accenture-led round means **some** strategic/corporate dynamics — read Bloomberg's coverage before your final-round interviews to speak intelligently about the partnership.
