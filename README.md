## TL;DR strategy

At 5 YoE, 50 LPA cash in India is reachable but above the median senior-SWE band. The realistic paths are:

1. **Well-funded Indian startups (Series B+)** — Razorpay, CRED, Zerodha, Postman, Rippling India, Zepto, Groww, Meesho, PhonePe, Navi, slice, Atlan, Rubrik India, Hasura, etc. Senior bands here often sit at 40–70 LPA cash.
2. **US/EU startups hiring remote engineers in India** — pays in USD/EUR, frequently crosses 50 LPA equivalent at senior level. Turing, Crossover, Toptal are aggregators; direct applications via LinkedIn/Wellfound beat aggregators on comp.
3. **AI-first startups** — AI/ML skill is a premium right now; applied-AI engineers at well-funded startups (Sarvam, Krutrim, Fractal, Neysa, plus global AI cos hiring remote) pay above market.
4. **Consulting/contracting** — 50–80 USD/hr remote contracts easily clear 3-4 L/month. Higher volatility, no benefits.

Don't rely on a single channel — a 50 LPA outcome usually comes from running 3–4 pipelines in parallel for 6–10 weeks.
